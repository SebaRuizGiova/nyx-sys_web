Who We Are

Nyx-Sys is a company dedicated to monitoring sleep performance to improve people's well-being.

We are pioneers in the field of sleep performance monitoring. Our mission is to provide people with the tools and information they need to optimise their sleep and overall well-being. Using our state-of-the-art, non-invasive device, we provide accurate, real-time data to help you understand your sleep patterns better than ever before.
Our platform is designed to be easy to use and accessible to everyone, making it easy to track, analyse and improve your sleep health. At Nyx-Sys, we believe that better sleep leads to a better life, and we're here to help you get there.

We measure

Objective physiological indicators during the sleep period, which determine training adaptation, recovery, performance and injury risk in high performance teams and athletes. Real-time monitoring of neuroautonomic, cardiorespiratory and neuromotor variables, latency time, quality and total recovery during sleep.

We use

The most advanced technology; our non-invasive biometric sensors perform automated monitoring. Located under the mattress, our ballistocardiography-based sleep sensor provides the most comprehensive data collected without contact, load or effort from the user.

We analyse

Our interactive platform offers a clear and simple data reading, which allows a quick interpretation of the user's condition, enabling a quick adaptation to effects such as jet lag, training corrections, nutrition, supplementation, recovery protocols, etc.

We assist

We prioritize personalized assistance to guide users through their sleep improvement journey. Our dedicated team provides expert insights and support, helping individuals navigate their data effectively. We empower users to implement tailored strategies for better sleep, ensuring they feel confident and informed every step of the way.

Our Expertise

On-Site Assistance

We travel with teams and groups to provide personalized support during the first days of device usage. This ensures a smooth implementation and adaptation for both users and technical teams.

Training

We provide personalized training with our professionals to ensure optimal use of the platform. We help users interpret sleep data and maximize the functionality of the device and the reports generated.

Personalized Reports

We provide daily, weekly, and monthly reports with detailed analysis of sleep and well-being. These reports help make data-driven decisions to optimize performance and recovery.

Collaboration with Medical Teams

We work closely with medical teams and coaching staff to deliver accurate, real-time information. Our goal is to provide reliable data that enhances training plans and recovery strategies.

24/7 Support

We offer 24/7 technical and specialized support. Our team is always available to resolve any issues or questions that arise during the use of the device and platform.

Continuous Updates and Improvements

We ensure that both the device and platform are always up-to-date with the latest technological advancements. Our development team works continuously to deliver new features and improve data accuracy.

NyxSys accompanied the Costa Rican National Team during their training week leading up to the decisive match against New Zealand, which secured their spot in the Qatar 2022 World Cup. Device test with Italian Seria A team ‘La Spezia’.

NyxSys recently conducted a device test with the Italian Serie A team, La Spezia. Over the course of several weeks, we worked closely with the coaching and medical staff to implement our non-invasive sleep monitoring technology, designed to optimize player recovery and performance. The feedback from La Spezia’s coaching team was overwhelmingly positive, with particular emphasis on the ease of use and reliability of the data. This collaboration with La Spezia not only demonstrated the efficacy of our technology but also highlighted the growing recognition of sleep as a critical component in elite sports performance.
